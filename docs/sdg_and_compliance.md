# 🌍 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) & Legal Compliance

CrisisLink is deeply rooted in social impact. We designed the architecture not only to save lives but to actively align with global humanitarian standards and legal frameworks.

---

## 🇺🇳 Alignment with UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

### 1. Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being
**Target 3.6 & 3.D:** Halve the number of global deaths and injuries from road traffic accidents, and strengthen capacity for early warning, risk reduction, and management of national and global health risks.
* **How CrisisLink Helps:** By enabling instant, community-routed responses to medical emergencies, accidents, and fires via the "Going" button, CrisisLink drastically reduces response times, directly saving lives when every second counts.

### 2. Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
**Target 11.5:** Significantly reduce the number of deaths and the number of people affected by disasters.
* **How CrisisLink Helps:** CrisisLink transforms isolated citizens into a connected, resilient mesh network. In earthquakes or floods, the hyperlocal awareness allows uninjured citizens to rescue neighbors, making the entire city highly resilient to sudden infrastructure collapse.

### 3. Goal 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions
**Target 16.6:** Develop effective, accountable, and transparent institutions at all levels.
* **How CrisisLink Helps:** The transparent, crowdsourced reputation and verification system actively fights misinformation and panic during crises, ensuring emergency data is democratized, accurate, and accountable.

## ⚖️ Legal Frameworks & Compliance

### 1. Good Samaritan Laws
Many jurisdictions legally protect citizens who offer voluntary, good-faith assistance to those injured or in danger. 
* CrisisLink acts as an enabler of Good Samaritan actions. By crowdsourcing incident locations and offering a "Going" button, we provide the platform for citizens to exercise their legal right (and moral duty) to assist without fear of undue liability, fostering a culture of mutual aid.

### 2. Data Privacy (GDPR, CCPA, & DPDP Act Alignment)
Emergency apps often fall into grey areas regarding data tracking. CrisisLink is built with Privacy by Design:
* **No Persistent User Tracking:** The app does not require a complex login system or profile creation.
* **Ephemeral Location Data:** GPS coordinates are used purely to calculate proximity (radius checks) and are not permanently tied to a user's identity. 
* **Data Minimization:** Audio recordings are stored specifically for the incident lifecycle to aid responders, and users are completely anonymized by default. 

### 3. Misinformation Laws
Many countries have strict laws penalizing the spread of fake news or inducing mass panic during disasters.
* CrisisLink employs **Gemini 2.5 Flash** as an active AI moderation layer to identify and drop prank calls, fake alerts, and spam before they hit the network.
* The secondary layer, the **Community Reputation System**, allows users to quickly "Fake" vote an incident, ensuring malicious actors are swiftly de-platformed. This dual-layer moderation ensures CrisisLink complies with laws requiring platforms to mitigate mass-panic misinformation.
